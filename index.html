Contract Review Sequence

  1. Start with dimensions, project type, and site conditions before estimating.
  2. Separate concrete quantity, base prep, delivery, labor, and special details.
  3. Use related references to compare assumptions before ordering material or requesting bids.
  4. Confirm final requirements with local suppliers, contractors, inspectors, or engineers when needed.
